Jam Yang

is a windowsill gardener and SFF writer. In the past, she wrote terrible puns as a game writer, but now she works in publishing, where she barely makes puns at all. Her short fiction has appeared in Lightspeed Magazine and Anathema: Spec from the Margins. Oh, and she also goes by Jessica.
